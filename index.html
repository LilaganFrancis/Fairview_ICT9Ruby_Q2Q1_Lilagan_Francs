Bitcoin (BTC)

Bitcoin is the first decentralized digital currency. Launched in 2009 by an anonymous person or group known as Satoshi Nakamoto.

Ethereum (ETH)

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that runs smart contracts. It was proposed by Vitalik Buterin in late 2013 and went live in 2015.

Ripple (XRP)

Ripple is a digital payment protocol and cryptocurrency. XRP is used to facilitate fast, low-cost international payments.
